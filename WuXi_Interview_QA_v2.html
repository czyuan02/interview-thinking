你能简单解释 logistic regression / decision tree / Cox 吗？ 如果面试官临时考你统计概念
▼

Logistic regression：用于二分类，比如 high-risk vs. low-risk。输出的是某个事件发生的概率，结果比较容易解释（某变量增加时，风险的 odds 是增加还是减少）。局限：类别不平衡时对少数类识别不好，不能只看 accuracy，还要看 precision 和 recall。

Decision tree：比较直观的分类或预测方法，通过一个一个条件把数据分成不同组。优点：容易解释，适合和非技术背景的人沟通，可以看到哪些变量影响判断。缺点：不控制深度容易过拟合，需要剪枝或限制树深度。

Cox 生存分析：用于 time-to-event 数据，分析某个事件什么时候发生及哪些因素影响风险。和 logistic regression 的区别：还考虑事件发生的时间，以及 censored data（研究结束时还没有发生事件的人）。因为有生物统计背景，这个模型的解释逻辑我比较熟悉。

Logistic regression: Used for binary classification — high-risk vs. low-risk. Outputs the probability of an event, relatively interpretable (does a variable increase or decrease the odds of an outcome). Limitation: may not identify minority class well with imbalanced data — need precision and recall, not just accuracy.

Decision tree: Intuitive classification or prediction method — splits data step by step based on conditions. Advantage: easy to explain to non-technical audiences; you can see which variables drive the split. Limitation: overfitting if depth is not controlled — needs pruning or depth limits.

Cox survival analysis: Used for time-to-event data — when an event happens and which factors affect risk. Key difference from logistic regression: also considers timing and handles censored data (people who have not experienced the event by study end). Familiar to me from biostatistics background.
